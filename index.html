HOW WE OPERATE:

  • 1

    Site Survey Building Project

    We will always come out and undertake a site survey before a quotation is provided. Meeting at site with the clients allows us to physically see and understand what the requirements are and how they can be achieved.

  • 2

    Architectural /Structural Design, if required

    If you decide that you would like one of our Architects/Structural engineers to produce existing floor plans, elevations, section and feasibility study including sketch layout options then we come up with a design. Once it is all agreed, we will include a full breakdown into the building quote.

  • 3

    Build Project Quatation

    Once the specification of the project has been outlined and agreed then we will discuss the project with relevant team members and put together a very competitive quotation, within an agreed timeframe.

  • 4

    Contract Awarded

    If KM Building & Construction is the company you would like to complete your project and we can collaborate with each other then we would draw up a contract which is normally JCT, or we are happy to work within a similar one. We also create a payment plan consisting of a deposit and invoicing thereafter- every two weeks on agreedwork completed.

  • 5

    Scope of works agreed

    Once the paperwork is agreed we then put together a weekly building programme which is reviewed every week on site. At this stage we would also agree a start date for the project.

  • 6

    Commencing Building work

    We manage the build from start to finish, taking care of all site hoardings, security and safety. Day to day work is carried out by our team whilst bringing in our regular subcontractors when required, for plumbing and electrical work only. We also manage building control making sure we get them on site at every stage required for inspections and sign offs to ensure a smooth running project.

  • 7

    Completion

    Once we completed our final site inspections, we will hand over your property to you with a full handover book and user guide for new items in your home. Snagging list is made at the time together with the customer to make sure anything he/she is not happy with to be rectified and brought to a required standard.Negotiate with the Building Control Department to ensure completion certificate is issued.

  • 8

    Retention

    We will agree a time period at the completion date meeting to undertake a snagging list for you and to pay the final retention money. Once snagging is complete the project is 100% completed.

Our experience allows us to manage the complexities of operating in any environment. As well as working closely with our clients, we enjoy excellent relationships with local authority officers. Communication is our highest priority, and you can be confident that any disruption to you, your family and your neighbours during building will be kept to an absolute minimum.
