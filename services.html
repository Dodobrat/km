Key Property Services

Project Management

Our project managers oversee our projects from beginning to end. They manage the planning, design and construction of our projects by supervising workers and keeping customers up-to-date with any developments that pertain to the project. The core role of our project managers is to ensure construction safety standards are met within the available budget and time.

Surveys and Inspections

We offer site surveys and inspections where we gather information on design and estimate on building costs. Our surveys include:

  • Testing and certification of electrical installations such as sockets and lighting switches.

  • Inspecting electrical appliances.

  • Testing portable appliances.

  • Conducting pre-building surveys to gather information on plumbing, central heating, electrical circuits, roofing and drainage systems.

  • Gas safety inspection which is conducted by our Gas Safe registered engineers.

Renewable Energy

KM Building & Construction is environmentally conscious and therefore, we give advice on the most appropriate renewable energy technology to use in your projects. We offer advice on use of the following renewable energy technologies:

  • Solar

  • Wind

  • Ground Source heat pumps

  • Air source heat pumps

The use of renewable energy technologies brings down expenses in a home or business. Our installers are all NICEIC Approved ensuring we adhere to construction safety standards.

Property Management

Do you own a flat, block of flats or are you a management company looking for a manager? KM Building & Construction offers property management services that ensure property owners live a stress free life. This we do by ensuring we communicate any issues pertaining to the property with our clients. Our services involve:

  • Leasing

  • Tenant Screening

  • Rent collection

  • Maintainance

  • Inspection

  • Evictions

Innovative materials and fittings supply

We source for state of the art and environmentally friendly materials and fittings for all projects we undertake. The materials we use are of the highest quality yet affordable for our clients.
